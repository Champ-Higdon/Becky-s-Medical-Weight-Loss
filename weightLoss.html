Medically Assisted Weight Loss

Semiglutide: Semiglutide is a GLP-1 receptor agonist that helps regulate appetite and blood sugar levels, making it an effective tool for weight loss. It is administered via weekly injections.

Tirzepatide: A dual-action GLP-1 and GIP receptor agonist, Tirzepatide enhances insulin sensitivity and reduces cravings, supporting a comprehensive weight loss program.

Lipotropic Injections (with B12): Lipotropic injections are designed to help your body burn fat more efficiently. These injections often include B12, which enhances energy levels and metabolic function.

Low-Dose Naltrexone: Low-Dose Naltrexone is a medication that helps regulate the immune system and has been found to support weight loss by reducing inflammation and appetite.

Phentermine: Phentermine is a prescription appetite suppressant that helps patients kickstart their weight loss journey by reducing hunger.

All of these treatments come with personalized medical counseling to ensure safe and effective weight loss tailored to individual needs.

All TRT plans start with in-depth assessment of labs. All medications are shipped discreetly to the patient's home from FDA-approved pharmacies.

Women's Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)

Patient care begins with discussing patients' challenges and symptoms which can include fatigue, low libido, hair loss, weight gain, depression, and brain fog. Complete lab panels are obtained to assess hormone deficits including testosterone, estrogen, progesterone, and thyroid levels, as well as excess cortisol. All medication is ordered and delivered to the patients' homes using reputable and FDA-approved pharmacies.
